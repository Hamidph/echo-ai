# Pricing Review

Current Echo AI pricing:

| Plan | Price | Prompts |
|---|---|---|
| FREE | £0 | 3 |
| STARTER | £35/mo | 10 |
| PRO | £55/mo | 15 |
| ENTERPRISE | £169/mo | 50 |
| ENTERPRISE+ | £599/mo | 200 |

Value metric: prompts/month (each prompt = one brand × one query × N iterations)

For "$ARGUMENTS" or general review:

1. **Competitor pricing**: look up current pricing for Profound, Peec, Otterly, Goodie
2. **Value metric check**: is "prompts" the right unit? Compare to seats, brands, API calls, reports
3. **Tier logic**: are jumps between tiers logical? (FREE→STARTER is a £35 cliff)
4. **Entry point**: is £35 too high for agency trials? Benchmark against similar SaaS
5. **Ceiling**: is £599 too low for large agencies? What do they pay for SEMrush/Ahrefs?
6. **Agency packaging**: should we offer a white-label or reseller tier?
7. **Annual discount**: should we add annual billing at 2 months free?
8. **Recommendations**: specific changes with reasoning and expected impact
9. **A/B test ideas**: what to test without a full reprice

Ground everything in competitor data and customer value delivered.
