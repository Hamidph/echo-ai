# Echo AI - Product Overview

## Value Proposition
**Echo AI** is the first visibility intelligence platform for the Large Language Model era. 

Traditional SEO tools track your ranking on Google. Echo AI tracks your **Share of Voice** on ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity. By running probabilistic experiments (Monte Carlo simulations), we tell you exactly how often and how positively AI recommends your brand.

## Use Cases

### For SEO Agencies
- **New Revenue Stream**: Offer "AIO (AI Optimization)" services alongside traditional SEO.
- **Audit Reports**: Generate PDF reports showing clients where they vanish in AI responses.
- **Keyword Research**: Discover what questions actually trigger brand mentions.

### For Brand Managers
- **Reputation Monitoring**: Detect when AI models hallucinate negative facts about your products.
- **Competitor Benchmarking**: See head-to-head win rates against your rivals (e.g., "Salesforce vs HubSpot").
- **Crisis Management**: Verify if PR fixes have propagated to LLM training data/context windows.

### For PR Teams
- **Impact Measurement**: Track how press releases influence AI knowledge bases over time.
- **Narrative Control**: Ensure your official messaging is what users see when they ask "What IS [Brand]?"

## Key Features
- **Monte Carlo Simulations**: We run 50-100 iterations per prompt to eliminate randomness and give statistically significant results.
- **Multi-Provider Support**: Test against OpenAI (GPT-4), Anthropic (Claude 3.5), and Perplexity (Sonar).
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Automated grading of brand sentiment in every response.
- **Share of Voice**: Percentage of time your brand appears in "Top recommendations" lists.
